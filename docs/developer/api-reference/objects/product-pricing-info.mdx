Represents availability of a product in the storefront.

```graphql
type ProductPricingInfo {
  onSale: Boolean
  discount: TaxedMoney
  discountPrior: TaxedMoney
  discountLocalCurrency: TaxedMoney
  displayGrossPrices: Boolean!
  priceRange: TaxedMoneyRange
  priceRangeUndiscounted: TaxedMoneyRange
  priceRangePrior: TaxedMoneyRange
  priceRangeLocalCurrency: TaxedMoneyRange
}
```

### Fields

#### [`onSale`](#) ([`Boolean`](../../../developer/api-reference/scalars/boolean))

Whether it is in sale or not.

#### [`discount`](#) ([`TaxedMoney`](../../../developer/api-reference/objects/taxed-money))

The discount amount if in sale (null otherwise).

#### [`discountPrior`](#) ([`TaxedMoney`](../../../developer/api-reference/objects/taxed-money))

The discount amount compared to prior price. Null if product is not on sale or prior price was not provided in VariantChannelListing

Added in Saleor 3.21.

#### [`discountLocalCurrency`](#) ([`TaxedMoney`](../../../developer/api-reference/objects/taxed-money))

The discount amount in the local currency.

#### [`displayGrossPrices`](#) ([`Boolean!`](../../../developer/api-reference/scalars/boolean))

Determines whether displayed prices should include taxes.

#### [`priceRange`](#) ([`TaxedMoneyRange`](../../../developer/api-reference/objects/taxed-money-range))

The discounted price range of the product variants.

#### [`priceRangeUndiscounted`](#) ([`TaxedMoneyRange`](../../../developer/api-reference/objects/taxed-money-range))

The undiscounted price range of the product variants.

#### [`priceRangePrior`](#) ([`TaxedMoneyRange`](../../../developer/api-reference/objects/taxed-money-range))

The prior price range of the product variants.

Added in Saleor 3.21.

#### [`priceRangeLocalCurrency`](#) ([`TaxedMoneyRange`](../../../developer/api-reference/objects/taxed-money-range))

The discounted price range of the product variants in the local currency.
